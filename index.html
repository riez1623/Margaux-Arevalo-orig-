Margaux Arevalo

By Riez Matthieu

It all started when I was just laying on my bed, I was so bored, my friends were all busy...

We started talking, asking each other's hobbies, we were getting to know each other already...

We started talking for a few more days and yeahhh... I really knew that I was into her...

We met up, and I was so nervous but it was fun...

After a few weeks, we lost that spark...

After a few more days, she blocked me on messenger, Instagram, and TikTok...

It has been 1 month and 27 days since we started talking...

March 28, I saw that she unblocked me...

And yeah, after that day, we didn't talk anymore...
